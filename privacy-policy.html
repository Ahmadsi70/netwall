🛡️ Privacy Policy for InternetGuard Pro

🔒 Privacy-First Approach: InternetGuard Pro is designed with privacy as a core principle. All data processing occurs locally on your device with military-grade encryption.

Last updated: January 2025

1. Introduction

InternetGuard Pro ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy and personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our advanced parental control and network security application.

2. Data Collection and Processing

🏠 Local Processing Only: All data processing occurs locally on your device. We do not collect, store, or transmit your personal data to external servers.

Data We Process Locally:

  • 📊 App usage statistics (for blocking functionality)
  • 🔍 Keyword filtering rules and settings
  • 📝 Blocked content logs (stored locally only)
  • ⚙️ App blocking preferences and configurations
  • 🌐 VPN connection logs (stored locally only)
  • 📱 Device information for app management

3. Permissions and Their Use

🔧 Accessibility Service

Purpose: Required to monitor text content in other apps for keyword filtering and content moderation.

Data Handling: No data is transmitted externally. All monitoring occurs locally on your device.

🌐 VPN Service

Purpose: Used for network-level app blocking and traffic control.

Data Handling: All traffic routing occurs locally on your device. No external data transmission.

📊 Usage Stats

Purpose: Required to identify and manage installed applications for blocking functionality.

Data Handling: Used only for app management features. No personal data collected.

📦 Query All Packages

Purpose: Used to display and manage installed applications for parental control features.

Data Handling: Required for comprehensive app monitoring. No external data sharing.

4. Data Security

🔐 Military-Grade Security

All data is encrypted using military-grade encryption standards and stored securely on your device. We implement multiple security layers including:

  • 🔑 Android Keystore integration
  • 🛡️ AES-256 encryption
  • 💾 SQLCipher encrypted database
  • 🔒 Secure memory handling
  • 🚫 No external data transmission
  • 👆 Biometric authentication for sensitive operations

5. Third-Party Services

🤖 Optional AI Features

AI-Powered Content Moderation: If you choose to use AI-powered content moderation, your content may be processed by third-party AI services. This is completely optional and can be disabled at any time.

Premium Subscription: For premium features, we use secure payment processors. No payment information is stored on our servers.

6. Your Rights (GDPR Compliance)

📋 Your Data Rights: Under GDPR and other privacy laws, you have complete control over your data.

You have the right to:

  • 📤 Access your data (export feature available in app)
  • 🗑️ Delete your data (clear all data option in settings)
  • ✏️ Modify your data (settings can be changed anytime)
  • 🚫 Withdraw consent (disable features anytime)
  • 📊 Data portability (export your settings and data)

7. Children's Privacy

Our app is designed for parental control and may be used to monitor children's device usage. Parents are responsible for ensuring compliance with local laws regarding children's privacy. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

8. Data Retention

All data remains on your device until you choose to delete it. We do not retain any data on external servers. You can clear all data at any time through the app settings.

9. International Data Transfers

Since all data processing occurs locally on your device, there are no international data transfers. Your data never leaves your device unless you explicitly choose to export it.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this policy. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
