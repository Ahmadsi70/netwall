Privacy Policy & Data Protection

🔒 Privacy-First Design: InternetGuard Pro is built with privacy as the core principle. All data processing occurs locally on your device with military-grade encryption. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information to external servers.

1. Introduction

InternetGuard Pro ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy and personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our advanced parental control and network security application.

Our application is designed to provide comprehensive digital protection for families and individuals, including app blocking, content filtering, VPN services, and AI-powered content moderation, all while maintaining the highest standards of privacy and data protection.

2. Data Collection and Processing

3. Permissions and Their Use

🔧 Accessibility Service

Purpose: Required to monitor text content in other applications for keyword filtering and content moderation.

Data Handling: No data is transmitted externally. All monitoring occurs locally on your device. This permission is essential for parental control features.

🌐 VPN Service

Purpose: Used for network-level app blocking and traffic control to provide comprehensive protection.

Data Handling: All traffic routing occurs locally on your device. No external data transmission or logging to third-party servers.

📊 Usage Stats

Purpose: Required to identify and manage installed applications for blocking functionality and usage monitoring.

Data Handling: Used only for app management features. No personal data is collected or shared.

📦 Query All Packages

Purpose: Used to display and manage installed applications for comprehensive parental control features.

Data Handling: Required for complete app monitoring and management. No external data sharing occurs.

🌍 Internet Permission

Purpose: Used only for checking app updates and security definition updates.

Data Handling: Minimal network usage for essential app functionality only.

4. Data Security

🔐 Military-Grade Security

All data is encrypted using military-grade encryption standards and stored securely on your device. We implement multiple layers of security including:

  • 🔑 Android Keystore Integration: Secure key management using hardware-backed security
  • 🛡️ AES-256 Encryption: Industry-standard encryption for all stored data
  • 💾 SQLCipher Database: Encrypted local database for all app data
  • 🔒 Secure Memory Handling: Protected memory allocation and data processing
  • 🚫 No External Transmission: Zero data transmission to external servers
  • 👆 Biometric Authentication: Optional biometric protection for sensitive operations
  • 🔐 Device-Specific Keys: Encryption keys tied to your specific device

5. Third-Party Services

🤖 Optional AI Features

AI-Powered Content Moderation: If you choose to use AI-powered content moderation features, your content may be processed by third-party AI services. This is completely optional and can be disabled at any time through the app settings.

Premium Subscription Services: For premium features, we use secure payment processors. No payment information is stored on our servers. All transactions are processed through secure, PCI-compliant payment gateways.

📊 Analytics and Crash Reporting

We may use anonymous analytics to improve app performance and user experience. No personal information is collected through these services.

6. Your Rights (GDPR Compliance)

📋 Your Data Rights: Under GDPR and other privacy laws, you have complete control over your data and privacy.

You have the following rights:

  • 📤 Right to Access: Export your data through the app's export feature
  • 🗑️ Right to Deletion: Clear all data using the "Clear All Data" option in settings
  • ✏️ Right to Rectification: Modify your data and settings at any time
  • 🚫 Right to Withdraw Consent: Disable any features or permissions anytime
  • 📊 Right to Data Portability: Export your settings and data in a portable format
  • 🔒 Right to Restrict Processing: Limit how your data is processed
  • 📝 Right to Object: Object to any data processing activities

7. Children's Privacy

Our application is designed for parental control and may be used to monitor children's device usage. Parents are responsible for ensuring compliance with local laws regarding children's privacy. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 without parental consent.

If you are a parent and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately so we can take appropriate action.

8. Data Retention

All data remains on your device until you choose to delete it. We do not retain any data on external servers. You can clear all data at any time through the app settings. When you uninstall the app, all local data is automatically deleted.

9. International Data Transfers

Since all data processing occurs locally on your device, there are no international data transfers. Your data never leaves your device unless you explicitly choose to export it or use optional cloud features.

10. Data Breach Notification

In the unlikely event of a data breach affecting your personal information, we will notify you within 72 hours of becoming aware of the breach, in accordance with applicable privacy laws.
